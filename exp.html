• GDP is the Gross Domestic Product of the country, per capita (per person) in current USD
  • JMP is the percentage of the population with access to clean drinking water. Here I technically show 100-JMP, or the percentage without access to clean drinking water.
  • Cholera and Malaria deaths is purely the number of people who died of cholera or malariain that country in that year
  • Diarrhea deaths are broken into all, perH and DALY. "all" is the number of people who died in that country from diarrhea, "perH" is that number per 100,000 people, and DALY is the number of "quality years of healthy life" lost due to diarrhea in that country
  • Number malnourished is what you think
  • Temperature is the projected temperature in that country in 2020 and 2040, as chanign due to climate change, based on some models.
  • WASH deaths are deaths due to exposure to unclean Water, Sanitation and Higiene
